With this ticket, you will be able to get access to floors 124 and 125 of the towering Burj Khalifa. While there, you'll be able to explore the building's observation decks, which offer 360-degree views of the sea, desert and Dubai. Additionally, you will have access to a multimedia exhibition about the building's history.

Why visit
